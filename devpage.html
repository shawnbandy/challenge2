About Me

Hey there! My name is Shawn Canavan. I'm 26 years old and currently live in Roanoke, VA. My daytime job is media production with Bramson Productions. Other than that, I love to cycle, hike, play video games, and cook.

My education consists of a B.S. in Biology from Mercer University and a M.S. in Biology from Georgia State University. While getting my M.S., I taught Genetics and Microbiology and wrote my thesis titled "2D Gel Protein Analysis of Recovered and Damaged Copper (II) Nitrate Treated S. cerevisiae Cells" After getting my M.S., I worked at Georgia Tech researching neurotrauma in the BME department. I started coding around February for fun, mainly doing C# problems on codewars.com and leetcode.com, figured out that I really enjoy it and wanted to take the next steps to pursue it as full time work.

Here's a couple of interesting facts about me:
  • I have a black belt in American Karate
  • I used to be semi-pro at Counter Strike: Global Offensive
  • My current personal best for cycling distance is 64 miles (100KM)
